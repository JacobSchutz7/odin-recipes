Butter Scotch Pudding

This self saucing dessert is easy to make and even easier to eat.

Ingredients:

  • Dark brown sugar
  • Plain flour
  • Baking powder
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Golden syrup
  • Cornflour

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees celcius.
  2. Grease baking dish.
  3. Butterscotch Sauce: Whisk sugar and cornflour in a bowl and set aside.
  4. Pudding Batter: Place sugar, flour and baking powder in a separate bowl. Which to combine.
  5. Add butter, egg, milk and golden syrip. Whisk until mostly lump free.
  6. Scrape into baking dish.
  7. Sprinkle butterscotch sauce mixture all over the surface.
  8. Pour boiling water over the surface as carefully as possible.
  9. Transfer to oven. Bake 40 minutes or until skewer inserted into the cake part comes out clean.
  10. Cut through pudding to reveal butterscotch sauce underneath.
  11. To serve, scoop building into a bowl then douse with butterscotch sauce. Top with ice cream!
