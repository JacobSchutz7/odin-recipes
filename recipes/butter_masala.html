Chickpea Butter Masala

Easy to make and easy on the wallet. This is a delicious vegan curry that everyone can get around!

Ingredients:

  • Brown onion
  • Red capsicum
  • Carrot
  • Chickpeas
  • Coconut cream
  • Tomato paste
  • Garlic
  • Curry powder
  • Garam masala
  • Spinach
  • Butter

Steps:

  1. Sautee onions, garlic, capiscum and carrot
  2. Add tomato paste, curry powder, garam masala and allow to bloom.
  3. Stir in coconut cream and chickpeas. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  4. Turn off heat and stir in butter and then spinach.
  5. Serve with rice.
